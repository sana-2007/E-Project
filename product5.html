Product detail

Headphones are audio devices designed to deliver sound directly to your ears, available in wired or wireless versions. Key features include noise-canceling technology, adjustable earcups, and long-lasting battery life for wireless models. They come in various styles like over-ear, on-ear, and in-ear, offering a range of sound quality and comfort levels.
