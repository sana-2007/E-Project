Product detail

An electric fan circulates air to cool and ventilate spaces, with types like desk, tower, ceiling, and box fans. It operates through rotating blades powered by an electric motor, offering multiple speed settings and oscillation for better airflow. Fans are energy-efficient alternatives to air conditioning, providing cooling with lower energy consumption.
