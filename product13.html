Product detail

A washing machine is an appliance used for cleaning clothes by using water, detergent, and mechanical agitation. It comes in two main types: front-load and top-load, offering various wash cycles and load capacities. Modern washing machines include energy-efficient features, smart connectivity, and quick wash options for convenience.
