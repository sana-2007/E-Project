Wash Programs:

Automatic machines come with a variety of pre-set programs such as delicate, quick wash, heavy-duty, and eco-friendly modes, allowing you to select the best cycle for different fabrics and laundry needs.

Automatic Function:

These machines feature sensors that detect load size and adjust water levels accordingly, optimizing both water and detergent use. Some models also have automatic detergent dispensers.

Product detail

An automatic washing machine cleans clothes without manual intervention, automatically controlling the water, detergent, and wash cycle.
It typically offers features like multiple wash programs, spin cycles, and automatic load sensing for efficient operation. Available in both top-load and front-load models, it saves time and provides convenience for everyday laundry tasks.
